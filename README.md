# AI Agency Curriculum

**Built by Sidd Chauhan — April 2026**

A self-directed learning system for going from zero to selling AI automation services. Built entirely from free resources — no paid courses.

---

## Why This Exists

The paid course I was looking at (AI Automation Society Plus) costs money. Rather than buying it, I reverse-engineered the curriculum structure and filled it with the best free content from the people who actually know this space: Nick Saraev, Nate Herk, Liam Ottley, Alex Hormozi, and Justin Welsh.

The result: a tracked, interactive curriculum I own, can update, and can work through at my own pace.

---

## What It Covers

8 modules, ~40 hours of free content, heavy focus on sales (my biggest gap).

| Module | Topic | Focus |
|--------|-------|-------|
| 1 | Start Here | Claude Code setup + AI automation foundations |
| 2 | The AI Partner Model | Business model, niche selection, service types |
| 3 | Build Your Portfolio | 5 specific sellable builds (Nick Saraev + Nate Herk approved) |
| 4 | How to Offer & Where | Local walk-in script + digital outreach channels |
| 5 | Outreach & Cold DMs | DM formula, LinkedIn sequence, cold email cadence |
| 6 | Pitch & Close | Discovery calls, demos, objection handling |
| 7 | Pricing & Packaging | Real pricing tiers, value-based formula, retainer structure |
| 8 | Scale | SOPs, retainers, subcontracting |

## Pricing Reference

| Tier | Type | Range |
|------|------|-------|
| Starter | One-time build | $1,500–$5,000 |
| Mid-tier | One-time build | $4,000–$12,000 |
| Enterprise | One-time build | $15,000–$50,000+ |
| Maintenance | Monthly retainer | $500–$3,000/mo |

**Value-based formula:** Find out what the problem costs them annually → charge 20–30% of that.

## How to Use the Tracker

Open `index.html` in a browser (or visit the GitHub Pages link below). Click a module to expand it, check off lessons as you complete them, add notes per lesson. Progress saves automatically to `localStorage`.
